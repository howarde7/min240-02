Design process

How this app has been designed and developed
Users thought that it would be good that the platform has a tagging system to keep track of different Bible studies, whether they are ones catalogued in the platform or are links to other websites. They also liked how the platform is free, as other platforms like RightNow Media are paid services, though those services provide many other services as well. Users also thought that a messaging system within the platform would be nice, but they are unsure if they would use it, as texting already exists to communicate with those in your Bible study group.
Mark is a business major with a minor in education trying to fiish up his last year of college. While doing all of this, he is also leading a Bible study group at his church and is in need of a plan. Busy and not knowing where to look, Mark used Living Water Studies in order to easily find a study that is perfect for his group!
The idea behind Living Water Studies is that users can search for specific Bible studies through a tagging system. So if Mark wanted a study abot parable then he could search that word and the studies that are tagged with "parable" or something similar will come up as a result. There will also be a function where you can look at user uploaded studies and chooe from those as well. Along with that there will be a fucntion that allows users to uploadbible studies, usually by sending in a link to the study of by sending in a pdf, powerpoint, etc. And finally, there will be a function within the app/website that works like a texting app or something akin to Facebook Messenger, so useres can communicate with one another, recommend studies, and to keep their studymates accountable to doing thte study.
